Meet Marvin the Magic Monkey

Marvin is the bravest, most magical monkey in all of Bananaville! With his trusty medical bag and endless supply of banana band-aids, he's on a mission to keep the jungle healthy and happy.

But there's trouble in paradise! Winston the Wicked Wolf has been poisoning the bananas, causing all sorts of health problems for Marvin's monkey friends.

üêí Did you know?

Poisoned bananas can cause cancer, immune diseases, and cardiovascular diseases in monkeys! That's why Marvin needs your help!

Our Mission

Save the Monkeys

Marvin delivers medical supplies to monkeys in need across the jungle. From banana band-aids to coconut casts, he's got just what they need!

Stop Poisoned Bananas

Winston the Wicked Wolf must be stopped! We teach monkeys how to identify poisoned bananas and find safe alternatives.

Educate Young Monkeys

Through fun, interactive learning, we're building a generation that understands, advocates, and cares for their health.

Build Community

Together, we can create a healthier jungle for all monkeys. Join our troop today!
